About your teacher

Hi, I'm José Etulain.

I'm a Spanish teacher and Literature professor from Bahía Blanca, Argentina — with over 10 years of experience helping English speakers go from hesitant to truly confident in Spanish.

I studied Spanish Language and Literature at university, and I've been passionate about teaching ever since. What I love most is watching someone's process — that moment when it all starts to click.

I'm a digital nomad and I teach from wherever I am — which means I bring a living, breathing experience of language and culture into every single lesson.
